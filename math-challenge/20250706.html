如图所示，抛物线$ y = ax^{2} + bx + c $与$ x $轴交于$ A $，$ B $两点，与$ y $轴交于点$ C $，顶点为点$ P $，直线$ PH $是抛物线的对称轴. 过点$ C $作$ CD /\kern -0.25em/ x $轴交抛物线于点$ D $，连接$ AC $，$ AP $，$ PC $，$ PD $，$ AD $. 过点$ A $作$ AM \perp AP $交抛物线对称轴于点$ M $，点$ M $的纵坐标为$ -\frac{1}{2} $. 已知$ \angle CPD = 90^{\circ} $，$ S_{\triangle ACD} = \frac{17}{12} $. 求抛物线的解析式.

由图可知：$ a < 0, c > 0, -\frac{b}{2a} > 0 \Rightarrow b > 0. $
易得：$ P(-\frac{b}{2a}, \frac{4ac - b^{2}}{4a}), MH = \frac{1}{2}, AH = \frac{1}{2} AB = -\frac{\sqrt{b^{2} - 4ac}}{2a}. $
$ \because AM \perp AP, $
$ \therefore \angle MAP = 90^{\circ}. $
由射影定理得：$ AH^{2} = PH \cdot MH $
$ \Rightarrow \frac{b^{2} - 4ac}{4a^{2}} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot \frac{4ac - b^{2}}{4a} $
$ \Rightarrow -\frac{1}{a} = \frac{1}{2} \Rightarrow a = -2. $
$ \because \angle CPD = 90^{\circ}, $
由对称可知：$ \triangle CPD $是等腰直角三角形.
$ \Rightarrow \frac{4ac - b^{2}}{4a} - c = -\frac{b}{2a} $
$ \Rightarrow b = 2. $
$ \therefore CD = 2 \cdot (-\frac{b}{2a}) = 1. $
$ \because S_{\triangle ACD} = \frac{17}{12}, $
$ \therefore S_{\triangle ACD} = \frac{1}{2} \cdot 1 \cdot c = \frac{17}{12} \Rightarrow c = \frac{17}{6}. $
$ \therefore $抛物线的解析式为$ y = -2x^{2} + 2x + \frac{17}{6}. $
